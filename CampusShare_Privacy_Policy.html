01

Introduction

CampusShare is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights regarding your data. By using CampusShare, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

This policy complies with the Indian Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act 2023), the Information Technology Act 2000, and international standards including CalOPPA.

02

Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Full name, username, and G-Suite email address
  • Enrollment number and gender
  • College ID card photograph and selfie holding the ID (for verification purposes only)
  • Posts, messages, and content you create on the platform
  • Trust scores and transaction history generated through your platform activity

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and approximate location (city level only)
  • App usage patterns and session data for improving the platform
03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes only:

  • To verify your identity and enrolment status at IIT Roorkee
  • To grant access to appropriate communities based on your verified gender
  • To operate the borrowing, knowledge sharing, and campus gig features
  • To calculate and display your trust score and badges
  • To send you notifications about meetups, responses to your posts, and trust score updates
  • To improve the platform and fix technical issues
  • To enforce our Terms of Use and prevent abuse

We do NOT use your information for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purpose beyond operating CampusShare.

04

Strict Prohibition on Misuse of Data

CampusShare explicitly prohibits the use of any user data for the following purposes:

  • Completing or assisting with academic assignments, homework, exams, or any graded submission on behalf of another student
  • Academic fraud of any kind, whether by users or by CampusShare itself
  • Selling, renting, or sharing your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes
  • Profiling users for advertising or targeting
  • Any purpose not explicitly stated in this Privacy Policy

Your college ID photographs and selfies are used exclusively for identity verification and are deleted from our active systems within 30 days of account approval or rejection.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to any third party. We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • With IIT Roorkee administration if required by law or in cases of academic integrity violations
  • With Razorpay solely to process Campus Gig payments (only transaction-relevant data)
  • With Firebase and Supabase, our infrastructure providers, who process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements
  • If required by a court order or applicable Indian law
06

Data Security

CampusShare implements the following security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • College ID photographs and selfies are stored in encrypted cloud storage with access limited to admin reviewers only
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plain text
  • Access to user data is restricted on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security audits are conducted

Zero tolerance policy: Any security breach, unauthorized access attempt, or data misuse by any party — including CampusShare employees or administrators — will be treated as a serious violation and addressed immediately. Users who discover security vulnerabilities are encouraged to report them responsibly to our contact email.

07

Data Retention

  • Active account data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • College ID photographs and selfies are deleted within 30 days of admin review completion
  • Post and activity data may be retained for up to 2 years for platform integrity purposes
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
08

Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to grievance redressal: Raise a complaint with us regarding data handling
  • Right to nominate: Nominate another person to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: support@campusshare.in

09

CalOPPA Compliance

In compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA), we confirm:

  • Users can visit our app anonymously until registration
  • A link to this Privacy Policy appears on the registration and login screens
  • This Privacy Policy will be updated when practices change, and the effective date will be updated accordingly
  • We do not honor Do Not Track (DNT) signals as there is no industry standard for DNT compliance in mobile applications
10

Children's Privacy

CampusShare is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. As all users must be enrolled students with a valid college ID, we do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover that a minor has registered, the account will be terminated immediately.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the application. Continued use of CampusShare after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
