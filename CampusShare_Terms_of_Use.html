Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using CampusShare, you confirm that you are an enrolled student at IIT Roorkee, that you have read and understood these Terms of Use, and that you agree to be bound by them. If you do not agree, you must not use the application.

02

Eligibility

CampusShare is exclusively available to currently enrolled students of IIT Roorkee. To register, you must provide:

  • A valid IIT Roorkee G-Suite email address (@dept.iitr.ac.in)
  • A valid enrolment number (8-digit numeric format)
  • A valid username that meets our naming standards
  • Accurate gender information, which will be cross-verified by an administrator against your college ID

Providing false information during registration constitutes a material breach of these Terms and may result in immediate account termination.

03

User Conduct

By using CampusShare, you agree to:

  • Use the platform only for legitimate peer-to-peer sharing, knowledge exchange, and campus gig services
  • Not impersonate any other student, administrator, or staff member
  • Not use the platform to harass, threaten, or harm any other user
  • Not attempt to gain unauthorized access to private communities (boys/girls community) through any method
  • Not post false, misleading, or fraudulent borrowing or gig requests
  • Honor all confirmed meetups and borrowing agreements
  • Return borrowed items in the same condition and within the agreed timeframe
  • Pay agreed gig fees honestly and promptly
04

Community Rules

CampusShare has three communities: Everyone, Boys Only, and Girls Only.

  • The Boys Only and Girls Only communities are restricted access and require verified gender confirmation by an administrator
  • Any attempt to access a community you are not authorized for is strictly prohibited
  • Content posted in any community must be relevant, respectful, and appropriate for a college environment
  • Spam, advertising for external products, or irrelevant content is not permitted
05

Trust and Reputation System

CampusShare operates a trust scoring system that tracks your behavior on the platform. Trust points may be:

  • Awarded for completing meetups, returning items on time, helping other students, and positive ratings
  • Deducted for missing meetups, returning items late or damaged, and negative ratings

CampusShare reserves the right to adjust trust scores based on verified reports of misconduct. Trust scores are visible to other users and are an accurate reflection of your platform behavior.

06

Security Deposit

For borrowing transactions, a security deposit in the form of trust points may be locked at the time of borrowing. These points will be returned upon successful return of the item. In cases of non-return or damage, the points may be forfeited to the lender.

CampusShare takes zero tolerance toward any attempt to manipulate or abuse the security deposit system.

Campus Gig Services

CampusShare allows students to offer and request skill-based paid services (Campus Gigs). The minimum transaction amount is ₹50. By using Campus Gigs:

  • You confirm that the service offered is legal and does not violate any academic integrity policy
  • CampusShare is not responsible for disputes between service providers and recipients
  • Payment is agreed between the parties and facilitated through Razorpay; CampusShare takes no cut from transactions
  • Gig services may not include academic dishonesty of any kind (see Section 8)
08

Academic Integrity

CampusShare strictly prohibits the use of its platform for any activity that violates academic integrity. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Posting requests for someone to complete your assignment, exam, quiz, or any graded academic submission
  • Offering to complete assignments, exams, quizzes, or graded work on behalf of another student for payment or any other consideration
  • Using Campus Gigs to circumvent academic integrity policies of IIT Roorkee or any other institution

Skill-based services such as designing presentations, creating graphics, editing videos, tutoring concepts, and explaining topics are permitted provided they do not constitute direct academic submission on behalf of another student.

Any violation of this section will result in immediate account suspension and may be reported to the IIT Roorkee administration.
